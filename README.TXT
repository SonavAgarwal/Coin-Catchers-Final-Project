Challenge: Kill enemies - when you are close enough, they follow you, so lead them into a spike;

            COIN CATCHERS 3D
========================================

Controls
- UP ARROW = move up
- DOWN ARROW = move down
- RIGHT ARROW = move right
- LEFT ARROW = move left

Gameplay
- catch all the coins you can before time runs out
- avoid the purple enemies
- avoid the red spikes
- you are unable to jump up blocks, fall into holes, and walk over liquids
- purple enemies will follow you, so lead them to the red spikes to kill them
- be sure to check behind walls and under spikes for coins
- if your way is blocked by an enemy or spike, you can try to move fast enough that they don't kill you
